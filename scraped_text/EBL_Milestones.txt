# Eastern Bank PLC - Company Milestones

## Overview
Historical milestones and achievements of Eastern Bank PLC from 1992 to present

## Key Milestones

**September 20,2025**: EBL has once again been recognized as one of Bangladesh’s most trusted and influential brands, earning the prestigious Superbrands status for the third time. This marks EBL’s third Superbrands achievement, having previously received the honor for the periods 2009–2011 and 2018–2020.

**August 21,2025**: EBL has been recognized as one of the Top 10 Sustainable Banks of 2024 by Bangladesh Bank

**July 19,2025**: Eastern Bank has launched the ‘SkyFlex Visa Prepaid Card’—Bangladesh’s first app-based social currency prepaid card—designed specifically for the digitally savvy youth segment. The new card was brought to market in strategic partnership with The Yours Truly and Visa.

**July 18,2025**: Eastern Bank PLC has been recognized as the ‘Best Bank in Bangladesh’ at the Euromoney Awards for Excellence 2025, marking the bank’s sixth win of this prestigious international honor.

**July 07,2025**: EBL, Mastercard launch 'world’s first' biometric metal credit card in Bangladesh, marking a significant leap forward in Bangladesh's payment technology landscape.

**June 16,2025**: Eastern Bank PLC. won most Innovative Digital Bank- Bangladesh 2025 at the International Finance Awards 2025

**May 13,2025**: EBL partners with Grameen Digital Healthcare Solutions to support Hajj pilgrims with Digital Healthcare Services. This is a pioneering initiative of its kind in Bangladesh’s banking sector.

**May 12,2025**: EBL has entered into a strategic partnership with the International Finance Corporation (IFC) to advance its climate risk management capabilities. EBL becomes the first commercial bank in Bangladesh’s banking sector to collaborate with IFC on enhancing climate risk management frameworks.

**April 24,2025**: Eastern Bank has been honored for the second consecutive year with the prestigious Digital CX Award for its outstanding Cash Management Platform, presented by The Digital Banker. This recognition underscores EBL’s continued commitment to delivering innovative and customer-centric digital banking solutions.

**February 27,2025**: Eastern Bank honors five organizations in five categories for their innovative, effective, and successful initiatives in meeting the climate change-related challenges in Bangladesh at the ‘EBL Climate Change Action Awards 2025.’ The EBL Climate Change Action Awards is an initiative of Eastern Bank in partnership with German, French, and Danish Embassies in Bangladesh.

**December 23,2024**: EBL becomes the first local bank to act as a Visa National Net Settlement Service (NNSS) agent

**December 22,2024**: To empower and inspire Bangladesh's growing freelancing community, EBL has launched an innovative ‘EBL Freelancer Ambassador Program’.  The initiative is expected to promote financial inclusion and to contribute to the nation's economic progress.

**December 17,2024**: EBL has been awarded Best Bank in the Private Sector in Bangladesh and Sustainable Bank of the Year at South Asian Business Excellence Awards 2024.

**December 12,2024**: Ali Reza Iftekhar, Managing Director and CEO of Eastern Bank PLC., receives ‘C-Suite CEO of the Year Award’ award at the “Bangladesh C-Suite Awards 2024” organised by Bangladesh Brand Forum

**December 12,2024**: EBL relaunches Mastercard Platinum Aqua Prepaid Card in vertical look

**December 07,2024**: EBL and SOS Children’s Village join hands for youth skill development

**November 27,2024**: EBL joins hand with the Bangladesh Investment Development Authority (BIDA) to set up BIDA Daycare Center (BDCC). It is an initiative aimed at enhancing the work-life balance of working mothers of BIDA and the Bangladesh Economic Zones Authority (BEZA) and National Skills Development Authority Bangladesh (NSDAB).

**November 16,2024**: EBL received four accolades at Mastercard Excellence Awards 2024.  The bank was awarded for excellence in Business (Innovation), Credit Business (Domestic), POS Acquiring Business, and Online Acquiring Business categories for 2023-24.

**November 14,2024**: EBL has won silver award in the Private Commercial Bank category at the ICMAB Best Corporate Award 2023.

**November 04,2024**: EBL and icddr,b partners in healthcare waste management and safe water access

**October 28,2024**: EBL introduces Financial Literacy and Entrepreneur Development Training program exclusively designed for women entrepreneurs. Curated by ADB and Bangladesh Bank, the online training program aims at empowering women entrepreneurs in Bangladesh. This self-paced training provides practical knowledge, assessments, and certification, helping women borrowers from small enterprises enhance their business success.

**October 05,2024**: EBL wins ICSB gold award for the sixth time at the 11th ICSB National Award for Corporate Governance Excellence 2023.

**October 03,2024**: EBL has launched EBL Women Payroll Suite- a first of its kind product in Bangladesh exclusively for women professionals.

**September 01,2024**: EBL launches Islamic Banking services

**July 19,2024**: EBL wins Euromoney Best Bank award in two categories: ‘Best Bank' and 'Best Bank for Corporate Responsibility' at the prestigious Euromoney Awards for Excellence 2024 held in London on July 18, 2024.  EBL is the first Bangladeshi bank to receive the coveted award for the fifth time.

**July 04,2024**: EBL launches revamped Skybanking app

**July 03,2024**: EBL has been recognised as the 'Best Climate Focus Bank' during the 24th National Conference and Green Expo, titled "Policy Frameworks for Enabling Renewable Energy Investment: A Global and Regional Perspective”

**June 24,2024**: EBL opens 1st ever ATM services at Dhaka metro rail stations network at Agargaon station.

**June 11,2024**: EBL launches off-shore banking deposit program

**April 28,2024**: EBL collaborates with Mastercard to launch the first prepaid card for medical tourism

**March 06,2024**: EBL launches Korea Business Desk to facilitate bilateral trade

**February 28,2024**: EBL gets Euromoney Trade Finance Survey 2024 award. EBL has been ranked No 1 "Market Leader" in Euromoney domestic category in the Euromoney Trade Finance Survey 2024.

**February 07,2024**: EBL is first to get Bancassurance License from IDRA for both life and non-life insurance companies

**January 24,2024**: Eastern Bank PLC. receives highest taxpayer award

**January 24,2024**: EBL and icddr, b partners for sustainable healthcare waste management

**January 24,2024**: EBL receives bancassurance business approval from Bangladesh Bank

**December 21,2023**: Eastern Bank commercially launches WEAREBL devices

EBL becomes the first local bank to receive the coveted award for the third time 2020, 2021 and 2023.

**November 27,2023**: EBL has clinched awards at the "Mastercard Excellence Awards 2023" in three categories. The bank won "Excellence in Mastercard Business (Innovation)" for its SkyTrip Credit Card, "Excellence in Mastercard Business (Innovation)" for wearable payment solution, and ‘Excellence in Mastercard Online Acquiring Business.’
